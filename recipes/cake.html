Chocolate Cake

Description

Delight in our decadent Chocolate Cake, a moist and fudgy masterpiece layered with velvety chocolate ganache, perfect for any celebration or sweet craving.

Ingredients

  • 1 ¾ cups all-purpose flour
  • ¾ cup cocoa powder
  • 2 cups sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup buttermilk
  • ½ cup vegetable oil
  • 1 cup heavy cream (for ganache)
  • 8 oz dark chocolate, chopped

Steps

  1. Preheat Oven: Set to 350°F, grease two 9-inch cake pans.
  2. Mix Batter: Combine flour, cocoa, sugar, eggs, buttermilk, and oil, beat until smooth.
  3. Bake: Pour batter into pans, bake for 30-35 minutes, cool completely.
  4. Make Ganache: Heat cream, pour over chocolate, stir until glossy.
  5. Assemble: Layer cakes with ganache, frost the top and sides, slice and enjoy.
