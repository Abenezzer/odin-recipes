Chicken Alfredo

Description

Savor the creamy richness of our Chicken Alfredo, where tender grilled chicken meets silky fettuccine coated in a luscious Parmesan cream sauce, finished with a sprinkle of fresh parsley.

Ingredients

  • 2 chicken breasts
  • 12 oz fettuccine pasta
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 cup Parmesan cheese, grated
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley, chopped

Steps

  1. Grill the Chicken: Season and cook chicken breasts until golden, slice thinly.
  2. Cook the Pasta: Boil fettuccine in salted water until al dente, drain.
  3. Make the Sauce: Melt butter, saut√© garlic, add cream and Parmesan, stir until thickened.
  4. Combine: Toss pasta with sauce, top with chicken slices.
  5. Garnish with parsley and serve warm.
